# Pricing Model

Estimated retail packages for a small-scale proof-of-concept.

| Plan | Price | Included Images | Included Video Minutes | Included Storage | Target User |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Starter | $9.99 | 500 | 10 | 5 GB | Creator / student test |
| Basic | $24.99 | 2,000 | 30 | 20 GB | YouTuber / small batch |
| Pro | $49.99 | 5,000 | 100 | 50 GB | Agency / researcher |
| Business | $99.99 | 15,000 | 300 | 150 GB | Small business / team |

## Overage concept

- Images: about $0.01 per additional image retail
- Video: about $0.10 per additional minute retail
- Storage: about $0.03 per GB/month retail
- Custom enterprise plans for large video, compliance, or dedicated review workflows

These are business-model estimates, not guaranteed AWS cost quotes. AWS pricing varies by region, service, media duration, resolution, and API usage.
