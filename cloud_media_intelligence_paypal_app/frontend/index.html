Upload After Payment

Choose a package, complete PayPal checkout, then upload supported image and video files. The backend creates secure S3 upload URLs and starts the media intelligence workflow.

Safety language: This app reports labels, speech sentiment, repeated phrases, and review indicators. It does not claim to prove someone is lying, high, guilty, or truthful.
